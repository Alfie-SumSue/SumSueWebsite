Ông Đèn is your fun but protective friend. He will light up your room like no other. He's here for a good time AND a long time.

Inspired by Ông Địa, Ông Đèn's many faces are all having a good time. Ông Đèn comes in two sizes and multiple designs.

Size L is 36cm in diameter, and Size S is 18cm in diameter.
Made from embroidery hoops, LEDs and special faces, the lamp can be hung, wall mounted, or simply left to his own devices, leaning anywhere.
