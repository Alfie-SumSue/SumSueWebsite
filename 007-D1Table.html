Furniture
Concept
Vietnam

A dining table with biased positions.

This table concept was sketched for a client who approached us looking for bespoke home furniture.
Designed to use wood offcuts from a local factory, the table is biased to one end and features long sides with irregular curves. During a meal, this subtly changes the relationships between people compared to a regular parallel arrangment.
