Collective Imagining: Hanoi 2030

Event
Ba-Bau AIR
August 2023

How do you want to live in 2030?

2030 is the finish date of Hanoi's current urban plan. Our event started with a simple question, discussed and imagined together over 2 hours and a 2mx2m map at ba-bau AIR's space in central Hanoi. The event raised questions and invited answers around empowerment, equality, accessibility, ecology, and myths of the city of Hanoi.
Thank you to everyone who came, and to ba-bau for hosting with us!
