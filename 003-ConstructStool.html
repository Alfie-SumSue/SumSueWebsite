Construct Stool

This is a concept for a kit-of-parts, found-object stool.

The stool is made up of brick, a square of wood, a towel and ratchet strap, all elements found in the street near the studio. The stool is made of waste, fully disassemblable, and explores a tension structure. We're interested in how a design can be made out of simple assemblage of generic elements.
